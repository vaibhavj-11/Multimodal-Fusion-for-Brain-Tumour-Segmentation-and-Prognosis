Brain tumors constitute a critical challenge in the field of medical diagnosis and treatment. The accurate delineation of tumor regions and the ability to predict their behaviour are essential for effective patient management and optimized therapeutic interventions. This research endeavour proposes a novel solution through the integration of multi-modal imaging and prognostic insights. Medical imaging, particularly Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), has revolutionized the visualization of internal structures within the human body. Different MRI modalities offer complementary information about tissue characteristics, such as T1-weighted, T2-weighted, and FLAIR images, each highlighting distinct features of brain tissue and pathology. Additionally, techniques like Diffusion Tensor Imaging (DTI) contribute insights into tissue microstructure and connectivity. However, the complexity of brain tumors demands a more comprehensive approach that extends beyond segmentation. This research capitalizes on the integration of not only multi-modal imaging data but also prognostic indicators encompassing clinical information. This holistic approach seeks to bridge the gap between imaging and clinical insights, enabling medical practitioners to make well-informed decisions tailored to each patient's unique circumstances. The core innovation of this project lies in the fusion of multi-modal data and prognostic information using advanced machine learning techniques. Deep learning architectures specifically designed for multi-modal data fusion enable the extraction of comprehensive and contextually rich representations. This research aligns with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Primarily, it addresses Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being, by advancing the capabilities of medical professionals to provide accurate diagnoses and prognosis predictions, which are fundamental for delivering effective healthcare. As the journey unfolds, this research aspires to revolutionize the landscape of brain tumor diagnosis and prognosis. By synergizing the power of multi-modal imaging, we aim to create a comprehensive solution that not only aids medical practitioners in accurate tumor delineation but also empowers them with the foresight to formulate personalized treatment strategies, ultimately leading to improved patient outcomes and a deeper understanding of the intricate dynamics of brain tumors.
